## Week 2 Deliverable 1

## Personalised Self-introduction Structure Template

### 使用说明

请基于以下结构，完成你自己的英文自我介绍。  
建议时长控制在 **1.5–2 分钟**。  
这一版的目标不是“说得高级”，而是先做到：

* 逻辑清晰
* 信息完整
* 表达自然
* 符合技术面试开场语境

你可以先严格按照这个结构说，再逐步个性化调整。

---

## Recommended Structure

### Part 1: Opening and Current Identity

这一部分的目标是快速告诉面试官你是谁、你目前在做什么。

**Template**  
Hi, my name is **[Your Name]**. I’m currently working as a **[Your Current Role]**, and my main focus is on **[Area 1]**, **[Area 2]**, and **[Area 3]**.

**Example**  
Hi, my name is Leon. I’m currently working as a DevOps engineer, and my main focus is on cloud infrastructure, CI/CD automation, and deployment support.

---

### Part 2: Background Summary

这一部分的目标是概括你的工作经验和技术方向。

**Template**  
I have around **[X years]** of experience in **[your field / industry]**, and most of my work has been related to **[technical focus 1]**, **[technical focus 2]**, and **[technical focus 3]**.

**Example**  
I have around five years of experience in the software engineering field, and most of my work has been related to infrastructure automation, Linux system support, and cloud-based deployment workflows.

---

### Part 3: Key Responsibilities or Strengths

这一部分的目标是让面试官快速知道你“最擅长什么”。

**Template**  
In my current or previous role, I’ve mainly been responsible for **[responsibility 1]**, **[responsibility 2]**, and **[responsibility 3]**.  
I’d say my strengths are mainly in **[strength 1]** and **[strength 2]**, especially when it comes to **[specific scenario / problem area]**.

**Example**  
In my current role, I’ve mainly been responsible for CI/CD pipeline maintenance, environment support, and deployment troubleshooting.  
I’d say my strengths are mainly in automation and problem-solving, especially when it comes to improving delivery efficiency and handling environment-related issues.

---

### Part 4: Project or Domain Exposure

这一部分不是展开讲项目，而是轻描淡写地带出你接触过什么。

**Template**  
I’ve also had exposure to projects involving **[project type / domain]**, where I worked with technologies such as **[technology 1]**, **[technology 2]**, and **[technology 3]**.

**Example**  
I’ve also had exposure to projects involving cloud migration and internal platform support, where I worked with technologies such as Kubernetes, Docker, and AWS.

---

### Part 5: Current Career Goal

这一部分的目标是自然地引到“为什么你来面这个岗位”。

**Template**  
At this stage, I’m looking for opportunities where I can further grow in **[target area]**, especially in a **[international / remote / fast-paced / product-driven]** environment.  
That’s also one of the main reasons why I’m interested in this opportunity.

**Example**  
At this stage, I’m looking for opportunities where I can further grow in cloud infrastructure and platform engineering, especially in an international and remote environment.  
That’s also one of the main reasons why I’m interested in this opportunity.

---

## Full Version Template

Hi, my name is **[Your Name]**. I’m currently working as a **[Your Current Role]**, and my main focus is on **[Area 1]**, **[Area 2]**, and **[Area 3]**.

I have around **[X years]** of experience in **[your field / industry]**, and most of my work has been related to **[technical focus 1]**, **[technical focus 2]**, and **[technical focus 3]**.

In my current or previous role, I’ve mainly been responsible for **[responsibility 1]**, **[responsibility 2]**, and **[responsibility 3]**. I’d say my strengths are mainly in **[strength 1]** and **[strength 2]**, especially when it comes to **[specific scenario / problem area]**.

I’ve also had exposure to projects involving **[project type / domain]**, where I worked with technologies such as **[technology 1]**, **[technology 2]**, and **[technology 3]**.

At this stage, I’m looking for opportunities where I can further grow in **[target area]**, especially in a **[international / remote / fast-paced / product-driven]** environment. That’s also one of the main reasons why I’m interested in this opportunity.

---

## Personalisation Prompts

请先补全以下内容，再整理成你的自我介绍版本：

* My current role:
* My main technical focus:
* My years of experience:
* My strongest technical areas:
* My most relevant project / domain exposure:
* My current career goal:
* Why this role interests me:

---

## Reminder

这一阶段请优先做到：

* 不要信息太散
* 不要一上来讲太细的项目
* 不要堆太多技术名词
* 先把“你是谁、你做什么、你擅长什么、你为什么来”讲清楚
