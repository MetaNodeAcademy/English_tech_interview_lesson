# Slack / Jira 更新模板包

## Status Update
- Quick update: I found that ...
- Just a quick update — I’m currently working on ___.
- I’ve finished ___, and I’m now moving on to ___.
- So far, ___ has been completed, and the next step is ___.

## Blocker Update
- I’m currently blocked by ...
- I found an issue in ___, and I’m investigating it now.
- At the moment, the main problem seems to be related to ___.
- I’m still checking the logs / configs / recent changes to narrow it down.

## Follow-up Message
- I’ll share another update in around ___ minutes.
- I’ll follow up once I have more information.
- I’ll keep you posted as soon as I confirm the root cause.
