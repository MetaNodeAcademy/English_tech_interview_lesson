## Week 2 Deliverable 2

## First-round Technical Background Expression Framework

### 使用说明

这个框架用于帮助你讲清楚自己的 **岗位背景 / 团队背景 / 日常工作 / 技术重点**。  
它适用于以下场景：

* Tell me about your current role.
* What do you usually work on?
* Could you walk me through your technical background?
* What kind of projects have you been involved in?

建议长度控制在 **1.5–2 分钟**。

---

## Recommended Structure

### Part 1: Team or Business Context

先交代你的团队主要做什么，不要直接一上来讲很细的技术。

**Template**  
In my current role, I work in a team that is mainly responsible for **[team responsibility / business area]**.

**Example**  
In my current role, I work in a team that is mainly responsible for infrastructure support and deployment automation for internal platforms.

---

### Part 2: Your Role in the Team

再说你在这个团队里的职责定位。

**Template**  
My role is mainly focused on **[your focus area]**, and I’m usually involved in **[task 1]**, **[task 2]**, and **[task 3]**.

**Example**  
My role is mainly focused on DevOps-related work, and I’m usually involved in pipeline maintenance, deployment support, and environment troubleshooting.

---

### Part 3: Day-to-day Responsibilities

这一段说“你平时到底做什么”。

**Template**  
On a day-to-day basis, I usually work on **[daily task 1]**, **[daily task 2]**, and **[daily task 3]**.  
Depending on the project, I may also help with **[additional task]**.

**Example**  
On a day-to-day basis, I usually work on CI/CD pipelines, release support, and issue investigation. Depending on the project, I may also help with configuration updates and cross-team coordination.

---

### Part 4: Technical Focus

这一段告诉面试官你接触的技术栈和工作重点。

**Template**  
From a technical perspective, I spend a lot of time working with **[tool / platform 1]**, **[tool / platform 2]**, and **[tool / platform 3]**.  
A big part of my work is related to **[technical area]**.

**Example**  
From a technical perspective, I spend a lot of time working with Kubernetes, Jenkins, and cloud-based environments. A big part of my work is related to automation and deployment stability.

---

### Part 5: Value / Impact

最后补一句你的工作价值，不然容易像流水账。

**Template**  
The main value of this work is to help the team improve **[efficiency / stability / delivery speed / reliability]** and reduce **[manual work / deployment risk / response time / operational issues]**.

**Example**  
The main value of this work is to help the team improve delivery efficiency and reduce deployment-related issues.

---

## Full Version Template

In my current role, I work in a team that is mainly responsible for **[team responsibility / business area]**.

My role is mainly focused on **[your focus area]**, and I’m usually involved in **[task 1]**, **[task 2]**, and **[task 3]**.

On a day-to-day basis, I usually work on **[daily task 1]**, **[daily task 2]**, and **[daily task 3]**. Depending on the project, I may also help with **[additional task]**.

From a technical perspective, I spend a lot of time working with **[tool / platform 1]**, **[tool / platform 2]**, and **[tool / platform 3]**. A big part of my work is related to **[technical area]**.

The main value of this work is to help the team improve **[efficiency / stability / delivery speed / reliability]** and reduce **[manual work / deployment risk / response time / operational issues]**.

---

### For DevOps / SRE

你可以优先从这些角度组织内容：

* infrastructure support
* CI/CD pipelines
* deployment automation
* release process
* environment troubleshooting
* system reliability
* monitoring and alerting

### For Web3 / Blockchain

你可以优先从这些角度组织内容：

* smart contract interaction
* on-chain data
* wallet / transaction flow
* backend integration
* security checks
* protocol-related features
* data monitoring

### For AI / Full-stack / Backend

你可以优先从这些角度组织内容：

* frontend-backend collaboration
* API development
* data flow
* backend services
* feature implementation
* model integration
* performance optimisation

---

## Reminder

这一轮只需要做到：

* 让别人能听懂你的工作背景
* 不要把每件事说太细
* 不要一上来堆太多缩写
* 先把团队、角色、日常工作、技术重点和价值讲清楚
