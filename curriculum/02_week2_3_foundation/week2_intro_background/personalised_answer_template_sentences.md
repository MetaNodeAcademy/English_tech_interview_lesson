# 文件三：初版个人回答模板句型（Personalised）

## Week 2 Deliverable 3

## Initial Personalised Answer Template Sentences

### 使用说明

以下句型不是让你逐字背诵，而是帮助你建立自己的“基础表达库存”。  
你需要结合自己的岗位方向和经历，把这些句型改成适合你自己的版本。

这一版句型主要覆盖三类场景：

* 自我介绍
* 技术背景讲述
* 基础技术表达

---

## Part 1: Self-introduction Sentences

### 1. Opening

* Hi, my name is **[Name]**.
* Thanks for the opportunity. I’m happy to introduce myself.
* I’m currently working as a **[Role]**.

### 2. Current Focus

* My main focus is on **[Area 1]**, **[Area 2]**, and **[Area 3]**.
* Most of my recent work has been related to **[technical area]**.
* I’ve mainly been working on **[type of work]**.

### 3. Experience Summary

* I have around **[X years]** of experience in **[field]**.
* Over the past few years, I’ve been working mainly in **[domain]**.
* My background is mainly in **[technical direction]**.

### 4. Strengths

* I’d say my strengths are mainly in **[strength 1]** and **[strength 2]**.
* I’m particularly confident when it comes to **[specific problem area]**.
* One area I’ve spent a lot of time on is **[technical focus]**.

### 5. Career Goal

* At this stage, I’m looking for opportunities where I can further grow in **[target area]**.
* I’m especially interested in roles that involve **[technical area / environment]**.
* That’s one of the reasons why this opportunity really stands out to me.

---

## Part 2: Technical Background Sentences

### 1. Team Context

* I work in a team that is mainly responsible for **[team responsibility]**.
* Our team mainly focuses on **[business / technical area]**.
* The team I’m in supports **[system / platform / product area]**.

### 2. Personal Responsibility

* My role is mainly focused on **[focus area]**.
* I’m usually responsible for **[task 1]**, **[task 2]**, and **[task 3]**.
* A big part of my work involves **[type of responsibility]**.

### 3. Day-to-day Work

* On a day-to-day basis, I usually work on **[daily task 1]** and **[daily task 2]**.
* In practice, I spend a lot of time dealing with **[technical issue / workflow]**.
* Depending on the project, I may also help with **[additional responsibility]**.

### 4. Tools / Technologies

* From a technical perspective, I work a lot with **[tool 1]**, **[tool 2]**, and **[tool 3]**.
* I’ve had hands-on experience with **[platform / language / framework]**.
* Some of the main technologies I’ve worked with include **[list]**.

### 5. Value / Impact

* The main goal of this work is to improve **[efficiency / reliability / delivery speed]**.
* This helped the team reduce **[manual work / risk / response time]**.
* The value of this work is mainly in making **[process / system]** more stable and efficient.

---

## Part 3: Basic Technical Explanation Sentences

### 1. Starting an Explanation

* In simple terms, **[concept]** is **[simple definition]**.
* The main idea behind **[concept]** is **[core idea]**.
* From my understanding, **[concept]** is mainly used to **[function]**.

### 2. Explaining Differences

* The main difference is that **[A]** focuses more on **[X]**, while **[B]** is more about **[Y]**.
* One key difference is how they handle **[topic]**.
* Compared with **[A]**, **[B]** is generally more suitable for **[scenario]**.

### 3. Giving an Example

* For example, when we **[scenario]**, **[tool / concept]** helps us **[result]**.
* A common example would be **[example]**.
* In practice, this usually means **[practical explanation]**.

### 4. Talking About a Challenge

* One challenge I’ve encountered is **[problem]**.
* The main issue was that **[issue detail]**.
* What made it challenging was **[difficulty]**.

### 5. Talking About Your Approach

* To address that, I first **[step 1]**.
* Then I worked on **[step 2]**.
* After that, I coordinated with **[person / team]** to **[step 3]**.
* In the end, we were able to **[result]**.

---

## Part 4: Natural Interview Phrases

* From my experience, ...
* In my current role, ...
* One thing I’ve learned is that ...
* A big part of my work is ...
* What I usually focus on is ...
* One key point here is ...
* In practice, ...
* The main challenge was ...
* To solve that problem, ...
* The outcome was ...

## Final Reminder

这一版模板句型的目的不是让你“背标准答案”，而是帮助你：

* 先把自己的表达主线建立起来
* 避免一开口就乱
* 逐步形成适合自己的技术面试表达习惯

先有结构，再做自然化；  
先能说清楚，再做高级化。
